Now I am a Postdoc at National University of Singapore (NUS), under the guidance of Prof. Jin-Song Dong and mentored by Prof. Jun Sun from Singapore Management University (SMU). Before joining NUS, I received my PhD degree from School of Information Science and Technology (SIST) at ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Fu Song at System and Software Security Lab (S3L). My research is motivated by a simple goal: towards robust and verified AI Systems. More specifically, to develop theories and tools to aid the construction of certified AI systems, with rigorous and provable guarantees of safety and security. I received my Bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT).
